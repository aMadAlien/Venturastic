Investment Focus
Media and Entertainment
Market cap is set reach 2.4 trillion by 2024.

Global media and entertainment are a $2.1 trillion industry consisting of filmed entertainment (movies, television, digital video), audio (music, radio, podcasts), publishing (newspapers, magazines, digital publishers), and video games (console, PC, mobile, eSports.).

Media Industry has gone through a major transition, led by internet distribution and changing media consumption habits, this has opened up new opportunities, bringing in new winners.

From an investment perspective, it is of a distinctive nature, as it is a recession proof industry, due to its non-correlated to general economic trends, and people will consume media and entertainment content.

P&A Funding
Venturastic will organize financing for producers to finance marketing and distribution (“P&A”) costs so they will be able to hire distribution companies to distribute their motion pictures on favourable terms.

Print and Advertising costs, are expenses incurred by a theatrical distributor prior to and during the promotion and exhibition of feature motion pictures theatrically.

These costs include various forms of advertising including newspaper and television & radio ads as well as the cost of prints of the film that are projected in theatres. Venturastic will arrange financing for print and advertising costs for the theatrical release of feature motion pictures. Venturastic will evaluate completed motion pictures for such investments and also makes forward commitments for P&A at the time of production financing – in instances where Venturastic is financing production costs or is a part of other production financing arrangements. Forward commitments as this will enable producers to obtain higher presales and GAP financing as part of their production financing structure.

Fashion and Luxury
Our targets will be Italian, French, US and other European companies with solid business fundamentals despite undergoing a temporary financial distress.

We will also invest in small and medium sized Fashion brands that have growth potential and ambition to grow, by working alongside families and managers to oversee generational transition and growth programs.

We would focus on companies with proven product, history and tradition, that are ready to restructure if required, reorganize and scale.

We would aim to be the first call for companies who want to reclaim their lost grounds, by providing these brands the opportunity to restructure their companies, helping them to take measured risks to exit the loss phase, to regain the agility of a start-up while operating with the conduct of a public company, all while laying the ground for the future.

Charter of Investment for M&A and external projects
  • Seeking companies that are both simple and complex, but with foreseeable, free-cash-flow-generator.
  • Seeking investment that have long-term sustainable competitive advantages, significant barriers to entry, and low risks of disruption.
  • Seeking business that won’t be affected by macroeconomic factors.
  • Seeking companies with large enterprise values that will be likely candidates for inclusion in the S&P 500 index in the future.
  • Seeking companies and investments that will benefit from being a public company in the future, bringing them broader access to the capital markets, but will prefer to create and invest in companies that are not highly reliant on the capital markets to operate and grow their businesses.
  • Seeking companies that may be led by entrepreneurs who are looking for a partner with our expertise to execute on the next stage of their growth.
